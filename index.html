Missus Piggy

How to Use

  1. Set a money goal (or five!)
  2. Connect your account to pay yourself from.
  3. Add a chore, task, or activity that you want to get done today, tomorrow, or this week.
  4. Set the amount that activity is worth. Maybe going to the gym means you pay yourself $10!
  5. Track the number of activities or tasks you completed, adjust, or delete them!
  6. Once you've completed all your tasks, treat yourself!

Note: Make sure you budget enough for the week according to how much you want to pay yourself. Nothing sucks more than not having the funds to enjoy that sweet treat!
